About Me

I am a (Ret.) United States Navy Veteran who is just starting out in the fast paced world of Web Development, I will be receiving my Full Stack web development certification from the SMU coding bootcamp in February 2019. I am reletively new to the world of Web Development but I look to expand my library of knowledge and skill in a short amount of time. I love challenges that are beyond my scope, the process of struggling through to the correct answer brings me a great sense of satisfaction.

I may be new to the world of Web Development but I am not new to the technology sector, after being Honorably discharged from the Navy in 2013 I entered the IT sector and have been working Desktop/Deskside support ever since. I have worked for major corporations (IBM Cloud, Nautilus Hyosung America Inc.) in the past and enjoyed every minute of it.

I have always had the desire to learn how to code but never had the time and resources to do so in the past, so when I was presented with the chance to formally learn I jumped at the opportunity. I hope to make a career change in the near future, doing what I really enjoy. So come on in and have a look around.
